This application aims to provide the users with the live statistics of ongoing possible covid-19 cases in India. 
It provides a overall counter of confirmed, active, recovered and deceased cases in India. Users can also view the state wise and district wise data. 
The feature of user's personalized watchlist is also added for the users who are interested in keeping watch on specific states of the country.
Users can also find information about covid-19 virus, its symptoms and do(s)/don't(s) to be followed by civilians.
